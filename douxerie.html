Disclipine

Naming and Brand Identity


Year

2020


Douxerie is an online pastry shop startup which needed a logo and label design. The project began with its naming, and dozen of words related to pastry has been researched. Online voting through Instagram story was conducted and resulted in the finalisation of the name, which is an acronym from Doux and Patisserie. Both are French words meaning sweet and pastry respectively.

The logotype uses simple hand-writing style to give home-baked sense without losing its modern feeling. The logomark represents cakes directly, and in another per-spective, it can also be interpreted as a box packaging for the cake, which the owner has the intention to make the packaging unique from competitors in the future.
