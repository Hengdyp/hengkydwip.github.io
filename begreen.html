A personal project originated from an optional project for admissions portfolio by CalArts, Be Green is a campaign featuring posters to motivate people to adopt a more eco-friendly life-style and to be a part of these significant challenges to create a better world for us to live.

The logotype is developed to create coherence between posters. It includes a monogram that derives from the very recognizable symbol, the sustainable icon.

The idea is to deliver the message to the general audience succinctly and visually. So the headline of each poster is designed to include the icon of the message.
