Sabila Craft Batam is a print and gift shop that creates custom-printed gifts. The shop utilises acrylic as its material in crafting gifts. Originating as a home business, the business then expanded to a shop. Following the expansion, a visual identity refresh was necessary.

The whole branding focuses on simplicity and elegance by maintaining the white and black colour palette that have been used before. The primary logo uses a contemporary, simple and elegant typeface as its logotype and monogram. The gift ribbon shape and acrylic material inspire the S monogram. It can be expanded to form a pattern for the wide range of its branding kits.
