Alttein is a travel agency that aims to provide the best travel experience to their audiences mainly Singaporean travellers who like visiting Batam. Previously named Travessel, the owner wanted a new name which is a brand new word that doesn't exist in the dictionary, is easy to pronounce and remember, and is also based on western words.

The final logo is a wordmark with a logogram in front replacing the 'a' letter. Inspired by the icon of a sailboat, wheel, sun, and pinpoint, the logogram reflects the company's attitude and motto in serving its customers.
